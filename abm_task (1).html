How this task works

Step 1 - fixation cross

A + cross appears in the centre of the screen. Keep your eyes focused on it.

Step 2 - face pair

Two faces appear side by side - one with a threatening expression and one with a neutral expression. They disappear after a brief interval.

Step 3 - probe

A probe symbol (◆ or ◇) appears where one of the faces was. Press F if it appeared on the left, or J if on the right.
